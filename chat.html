Before You Start Chatting

Messaging Guidelines

Please read and agree to these before sending your first message. You'll only see this once.

  • Remain respectful and honest in every conversation, whether or not it progresses.
  • Keep correspondence within appropriate Islamic bounds until families are formally involved.
  • Do not share external contact details (email, phone number, social media) here — for your own safety, and so families can be properly introduced before any of that is exchanged.
  • If a conversation isn't right for you, say so kindly rather than disappearing without word.

Please Note

  • Messages that appear to share contact details are automatically flagged for our review.
  • Harassment, disrespect, or anything against our Community Guidelines can be reported directly from any message.
  • We reserve the right to review flagged conversations and take appropriate action, including suspending accounts.
