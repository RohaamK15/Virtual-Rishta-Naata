1Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years old, single and legally free to marry, and genuinely seeking a marriage partner to use Virtual Rishta Naata. By creating a profile, you confirm that everything you tell us about yourself — your age, marital history, circumstances, and background — is true and complete.

2The Nature of This Service

Virtual Rishta Naata is a platform that lets members create a profile, search for others, and make contact through the platform's in-app messaging. We are a facilitator, not a matchmaker, marriage bureau, or guarantor of any outcome. We do not introduce members to one another, recommend matches, or take any part in the conversations, meetings, or decisions that follow. Whether and how a connection develops — and whether it leads anywhere — is entirely between the members involved and, where relevant, their families.

3Self-Reported Information & Your Duty to Verify

Every detail on a member's profile — including whether they are an Ahmadi Muslim, their local Jamaat, their marital history, their qualifications, and everything else — is entered by that member themselves. We ask the question; we do not independently check the answer.

We do not conduct background checks, identity verification, or religious verification on any member. We are not responsible for confirming that any member is genuinely an Ahmadi Muslim, or that any other information on their profile is accurate. Once a member has told us they are an Ahmadi Muslim, verifying that claim — and carrying out whatever due diligence you consider appropriate on anyone you are in contact with — is entirely your own responsibility.

We strongly encourage you to take the same care you would in any other route to marriage: involve your family, ask questions directly, seek references through your own community connections, and take your time before making any decisions.

4Membership, Billing & Cancellation

  • The Monthly plan (£10/month) renews automatically each month until you cancel.
  • The Annual plan (£100/year) is billed once for a full year and does not auto-renew; we'll ask if you'd like to continue at the end of the term.
  • Cancelling stops future charges but does not refund the current billing period.
  • We do not offer refunds for accounts removed due to a breach of these Terms or our Community Guidelines.

5When Your Rishta Is Settled

If your Rishta is settled — whether through this platform or otherwise — it is your responsibility to tell us. Once you let us know, we will remove your profile and personal information from our database. Subscriptions do not cancel automatically just because you've found a match: if you do not tell us and do not cancel, your subscription will continue to renew and you will continue to be charged.

You can let us know by deactivating your account from your account page, or by emailing us directly. We're happy for this to be a happy email to write.

  • Sharing another member's details or profile information outside this platform without their consent.
